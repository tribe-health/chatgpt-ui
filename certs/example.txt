IMPORTANT: Please place the following files in this 'certs' folder:

1. privkey.pem - The private key for your domain, provided by Let's Encrypt.
2. fullchain.pem - The full certificate chain for your domain, provided by Let's Encrypt.

These files are required to enable HTTPS for your application. Make sure to keep your private key secure and never share it with anyone.

For more information on obtaining the necessary certificates from Let's Encrypt, please visit their documentation:
https://letsencrypt.org/docs/
